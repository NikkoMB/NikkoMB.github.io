Pokégachi

Pokemon themed application that allows you to choose one Pokemon. Once chosen, you will need to feed the Pokemon rare candy in order to have it evolve. It takes 5 rare candies to evolve the first time and 10 rare candies in order to evolve to the last Pokemon stage. Application’s server side is rendered by Node.js/Express.js and deployed by Heroku.
