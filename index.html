About Me
My name is Nikko Bustillo. I am full stack developer but, I specialize in creating beautiful, professional websites through front-end coding. I learned how to code through the Rutgers University Coding Bootcamp and continued working on beautiful projects after graduation.

With that said, I love coding and sharing my passion with my employer.
